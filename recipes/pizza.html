Pizza

Prep time: 30mins/ Cook time: 10mins/ Servings: 16/ total time 12hrs45mis

Description

This simple brick-oven pizza recipe has been made famous by several well-known, Brooklyn wood-fire pizzerias. Best accompanied by ice-cold, pilsner-style lager beer.

Ingredients

Dough

  • ¼ cup warm water
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 3 cups bread flour

Toppings

  • 6 ounces low moisture mozzarella cheese, thinly sliced
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ¼ teaspoon fresh ground black pepper
  • ½ cup no salt added canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Knead the dough
  3. Divide in half and form balls
  4. Make the pizza
  5. Preheat oven 550 F
  6. Prepare the first pizza
  7. Lay half the mozzarella on the crust
  8. Put the pizza in oven
  9. Cook for 4 to 6 minutes
  10. Remove pizza form the oven, add basil leaves
